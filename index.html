    Welcome to heber!

    Heber Valley, a little city inbetween the Great Salt Lake and other larger cities. Welcome!
    Heber City is happy to home just under 20,000 people, with various hotels scattered around, and tons of family business and restaurants.

    Location and Residency

    Heber Valley is located right next to Orem and Provo, and just near Salt Lake City in Utah.
    With about 20,000 people in the town, it's constantly extending its borders and buildings, including expanding into the mountains with great views, and possibly adding tourist attractions.

    Hotels

    Hotels in Heber have always been a bit difficult to search for, but here are some options!
    From lodges to fancy hotels, the hotels here span in prices from $66 to over $200.
    Our top pick, which can start from $172 to $200, is Daniels Summit Lodge. With a wonderful 9.2 star rating, they have a hot tub, pool, and a classic woodsy home feel.
    Another great choice for you and your family or significant other, is the Holiday Inn Express, an IHG Hotel, starting at about $140. An excellent 8.6 star rating, this hotel has a pool, gym, and is just at the edge of town.
