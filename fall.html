    Swiss days

    Swiss days is an event during August or September that includes both Heber City and Midway! It's planned by Midway, but a lot of residents from heber go for a ton of fun.
    Swiss days is an unique festival celebrating our swedish heritage throughout the towns. There are events like a parade, vendors, Swiss Miss, a mile run, and so much more.

    Early snow

    Snow is one of the most memorable things about Heber.
    It's always so nice to go with family somewhere and see how the natural weather affects the town around us, so why not go during the fall for that exact reason?
    Snow can start falling as early as October, making even Halloween feel as special as Christmas. It can range from just 1-2" or even fall to be several feet of snow.
